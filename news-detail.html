First Visit

How to prepare for first visit?
If you've never been to a physiotherapist before, no issues, there has to be a first time sometime.
The first visit is extremely important because it is the moment when we get to know each other.

We really want the patient to feel at ease, so we encourage you to prepare properly for the visit. It will certainly facilitate the diagnostic process.

What should you bring with you?

  • Medical documentation ( e.g. X-ray, ultrasound, magnetic resonance imaging, hospital discharges, etc.
  • Sports outfit that will ensure freedom during examination (it can be t-shirt, shorts/trousers)
  • Variable footwear (Sports, Flip-flops)

See you at the clinic.
